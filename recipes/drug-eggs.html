Korean Marinated "Drug Eggs"

Description

Korean Marinated "Drug Eggs" are a favorite in our household because of the tasty marinade and the jammy center of the soft-boiled egg. Fortunately, they are incredibly easy and quick to make - the longest part is the wait for the eggs to marinade!

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 scallion, chopped finely
  • 1 tablespoon Korean Red Pepper Flakes, also known as Gochugaru
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1-2 thai chilis
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 3-4 cloves minced garlic

Steps

  1. Fill a medium to large pot 2/3 of the way up with water and bring to a boil.
  2. Gently place 6 eggs into the water and boil for 7 minutes. For a more cooked center, boil for a few minutes longer.
  3. While the eggs are boiling, combine remaining ingredients.
  4. Once the eggs have finished boiling, take them out of the boiling water and put them in a large bowl filled with ice water.
  5. Peel the boiled eggs, and place them in the marinade.
  6. Cover the bowl with eggs and marinade and refrigerate for at least 6 hours before enjoying.
