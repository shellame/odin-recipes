Stir-Fried Egg and Tomato

Description

This was a favorite childhood dish of mine growing up. Whenever we had "culture days" at school, I would ask my mom to make this dish, but she always refused, saying that it wouldn't be to American taste. I love making this with ripe heirloom tomatoes during the peak of their summer season. Depending on the flavor of the tomatoes, you may not need to add sugar at all!

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Xiaoxing wine (optional)
  • 1-2 Tablespoons sugar
